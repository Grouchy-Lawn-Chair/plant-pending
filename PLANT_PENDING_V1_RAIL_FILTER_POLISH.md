# Plant Pending v1.0 – rail/filter/card polish

## Changes

- Plant card size text is larger and bold.
- Plant card sun/water icons are larger.
- Water icon is now a three-drop scale: one filled = low, two = medium, three = high.
- Added plant library filters for low, medium, and high water needs.
- Right inspector rail now has a separate Selected item panel.
- Clicking the active right-rail icon closes that panel.
- Clicking another right-rail icon swaps panels instead of stacking content.
- Selected item no longer sits on top of the Zones panel.
- Added keyboard zoom shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd + plus, Ctrl/Cmd + minus, Ctrl/Cmd + 0.

## Test checklist

- Select a plant and confirm the right rail opens Selected item.
- Click Zones and confirm Selected item closes.
- Click Zones again and confirm the panel closes.
- Check plant card water icon: low/medium/high should show 1/2/3 filled drops.
- Try water filters in the filter panel.
- Try Ctrl/Cmd + plus/minus/0 for zoom.
