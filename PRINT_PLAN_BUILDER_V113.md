# Plant Pending v113 - Print Plan Builder

## Added

- New print plan builder replacing the simple print preview.
- Ledger-size 11x17 landscape print CSS.
- Master plan page with full map, zones, legend, scale status, and total plant estimate.
- Zone detail sheet for each drawn zone.
- Zone mini maps cropped to the selected zone.
- Plant schedule / shopping list with quantities, symbols, sizes, sun, water, unit price, and subtotal.
- Cost estimate total and by zone.
- Plant photo sheet.
- Notes / warnings page when needed.

## Notes

Prices are planning estimates from Green Acres catalog data where available. The print view keeps the app's plan symbols and uses legend numbers for printed readability.
